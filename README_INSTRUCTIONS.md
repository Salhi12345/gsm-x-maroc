## How to Use This Package

### Step 1: Review the Listings
Open **facebook_marketplace_listings.md** and review all 10 listings. Each listing includes:
- Title (in Arabic)
- Price (varied from 150-450 MAD)
- Category
- Detailed description in Arabic
- Product tags
- Location and delivery info

### Step 2: Upload to Facebook Marketplace

For each listing:

1. Go to **Facebook Marketplace** (facebook.com/marketplace)
2. Click **"Create New Listing"**
3. Select **"Product for Sale"**
4. Fill in the details:
   - **Title**: Copy from the listing
   - **Price**: Enter the MAD amount
   - **Category**: Electronics > Software / Services
   - **Condition**: New
   - **Description**: Copy the full Arabic description
   - **Photos**: Upload the corresponding image from `marketplace_images/`
   - **Location**: Ø§Ù„Ø¯Ø§Ø± Ø§Ù„Ø¨ÙŠØ¶Ø§Ø¡
   - **Tags**: Add the product tags listed

5. Click **"Next"** and then **"Publish"**

### Step 3: Spacing Your Posts

**IMPORTANT**: Do NOT post all 10 listings at once!

Facebook's algorithm may flag multiple similar listings as spam. Instead:
- Post 2-3 listings per day
- Space them out over 4-5 days
- Wait at least 4-6 hours between posts
- Rotate which listings you use

Recommended schedule:
- **Day 1**: Listings 1, 2
- **Day 2**: Listings 3, 4, 5
- **Day 3**: Listings 6, 7
- **Day 4**: Listings 8, 9
- **Day 5**: Listing 10

## Troubleshooting

### If Your Listing Gets Flagged:
- Remove and repost with different wording
- Use a different listing variation
- Wait 24-48 hours before reposting
- Contact Facebook support if needed

### If You're Not Getting Views:
- Improve your product tags
- Adjust pricing
- Update images
- Boost the listing (paid promotion)
- Share in relevant Facebook groups

### If Customers Ask Technical Questions:
- Be honest about capabilities
- Offer to demonstrate if local
- Provide references or testimonials
- Share tutorial videos (if available)
